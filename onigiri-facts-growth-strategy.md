## Growth Strategy

### Phase 1: Foundation (Week 1-2)

#### 1. Profile Optimization
- **New Bio:** "🧠 Daily facts that will ruin your childhood 👁️ New fact every day ⬇️ Follow before you know too much"
- **Profile pic:** Bold, recognizable (maybe animated eye/brain icon)
- **Pinned video:** Best performing video (the 897 view one)

#### 2. Content Format Upgrade
Switch from text-only to:
- **Format A:** Green screen with fact text overlay
- **Format B:** POV style with hands/objects
- **Format C:** Reaction to own facts (face on screen)

#### 3. Hashtag Strategy
Use 12-15 hashtags per post:
```
#sciencefacts #grossfacts #didyouknow #mindblown 
#humanbody #biology #weirdfacts #factsdaily 
#learnontiktok #science #themoreyouknow #fyp 
#foryou #viral #trending
```

#### 4. Hook Formulas (First 2 seconds)
- "This is disgusting but..."
- "You don't want to know this but..."
- "Your body is hiding something..."
- "Scientists discovered something gross..."
- "I wish I didn't know this..."

#### 5. CTA Formulas (Last 2 seconds)
- "Follow for more nightmares"
- "Tag someone who needs to know this"
- "You'll never look at [X] the same way"
- "Want more? Follow"

### Phase 2: Content Strategy (Week 3-4)

#### 1. Posting Schedule
- **Frequency:** 2-3 videos per day
- **Best times (AEDT):** 7-9am, 12-2pm, 7-10pm
- **Consistency:** Same times daily

#### 2. Content Pillars (rotate between these)
- **Gross Body Facts** (40%) - mites, bacteria, stomach acid
- **Mind-Blowing Science** (30%) - space, physics, nature
- **Debunking Myths** (20%) - goldfish memory, etc.
- **Trending Sounds** (10%) - adapt to viral audio

#### 3. Engagement Tactics
- Reply to EVERY comment within 1 hour
- Ask questions in captions: "Did you know this?"
- Create reply videos to comments
- Duet/stitch other science accounts

#### 4. Audio Strategy
- Use trending sounds (check Creative Center weekly)
- Create original sound for brand recognition
- Mix trending + original 50/50

### Phase 3: Growth Hacking (Month 2)

#### 1. Series Content
Create recurring series:
- "Things living on your body" (episodic)
- "Facts that will ruin your day"
- "Science vs What You Were Told"

#### 2. Collaborate
- Duet other science fact accounts
- Comment on large accounts early
- Create content that's easy to stitch

#### 3. Cross-Promotion
- Post same content to Instagram Reels
- Create YouTube Shorts from best videos
- Share to Reddit (r/todayilearned, r/interestingasfuck)

#### 4. Trend Jacking
- Monitor trending topics daily
- Adapt trending sounds to science facts niche
- Jump on viral challenges with a science twist

## Key Takeaways

1. **Views are fine, conversion is the problem** - Focus on CTAs and value proposition
2. **Consistency beats quality** - Post 2-3x daily
3. **Engagement drives algorithm** - Reply to every comment
4. **Hooks make or break videos** - First 2 seconds are critical
5. **Niche down harder** - "Gross science facts" is good, lean into it
